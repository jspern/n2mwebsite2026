## Your Data Is a Target

Every business holds valuable data — financial records, client information, operational systems. Cyber threats don't discriminate by company size. In fact, small and mid-size businesses are increasingly targeted precisely because they're seen as easier entry points.

N2M Technology takes a comprehensive, layered approach to cybersecurity. We don't just install a firewall and walk away. We architect your defenses, monitor your environment, and respond when threats emerge.

## What We Do

We employ a full range of technologies and practices to keep your business secure:

**Network Defense** — Strong firewalls, secure network configurations, and network segmentation that limits the blast radius of any incident.

**Endpoint Protection** — Every device connected to your network is a potential entry point. We deploy and manage endpoint detection and response tools that catch threats before they spread.

**Encryption** — Sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring that even if data is intercepted, it's unreadable.

**Patch Management** — The most common attack vectors exploit known vulnerabilities in unpatched software. We keep every system current.

**Vulnerability Assessments** — Regular audits identify weaknesses before attackers do. We assess, report, and remediate — then verify the fix.

## Proactive, Not Reactive

The difference between a minor incident and a catastrophic breach is often whether someone was watching. N2M monitors your environment continuously, so threats are caught early — and often before you even know anything happened.
