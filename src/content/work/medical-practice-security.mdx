## The Challenge

A growing medical practice with three locations was operating with inconsistent IT setups at each site, no centralized backup strategy, and significant HIPAA compliance exposure. Their previous IT provider had handled issues reactively, and the practice had no clear picture of where their risks lay.

## What N2M Delivered

N2M conducted a thorough assessment of all three locations and delivered a unified IT and security plan. This included standardizing hardware and software across locations, implementing an encrypted cloud backup solution with documented recovery procedures, and deploying endpoint protection with centralized management.

Security policies, access controls, and audit logging were configured to meet HIPAA requirements, and staff received training on data handling practices.

## The Result

The practice now operates with consistent, documented IT across all locations. Their IT environment is actively monitored, compliance documentation is maintained, and their team has a direct support line for any issues. "Our IT program now runs seamlessly," the practice administrator reported.
