## Your Network Is the Foundation

Every other technology service your business relies on — internet access, cloud applications, phone systems, security cameras — runs over your network. A poorly designed or aging infrastructure creates problems that no amount of software can solve.

N2M Technology designs and installs network infrastructure that performs reliably today and scales cleanly as your business grows.

## What We Build

**Structured Cabling** — Clean, organized, properly labeled cable runs using Cat6 or Cat6A for copper, with fiber options for high-bandwidth or long-distance runs. Done right, a cabling installation lasts decades.

**Network Architecture** — We don't just run cables. We design the network topology — VLANs, switching layers, routing, and segmentation — to match how your business actually operates.

**Wireless Coverage** — Modern businesses need reliable Wi-Fi everywhere. We survey your space, design coverage zones, and deploy enterprise-grade access points with proper authentication and segmentation.

**Server Room & Data Closet** — How your equipment is organized matters. We build clean, documented server rooms and data closets that are easy to manage, easy to troubleshoot, and properly cooled and powered.

**Documentation** — Every infrastructure engagement produces complete documentation: cable maps, network diagrams, equipment inventory, and IP addressing. You own it, and it's kept current.

## Built to Last

Infrastructure work is an investment. We design for longevity — using quality materials, following industry standards, and building in the capacity you'll need as your team and technology needs grow.
