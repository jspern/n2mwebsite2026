## AI That Actually Works for Your Business

Artificial intelligence is not a future technology — it is available now, and N2M is already putting it to work for clients across Metro Detroit. We cut through the noise and focus on one question: *where is your team spending time on manual, repetitive work that a well-built automation could handle?*

The answer is almost always the same: document processing, data entry, status reporting, scheduling, and communication routing. These are solvable problems. And solving them frees your people to focus on the work that actually requires human judgment.

## What We Automate

**Document Processing** — Invoices, contracts, intake forms, and reports — we build automations that extract, validate, and route document data without manual handling.

**Workflow Automation** — Approvals, notifications, task assignments, and status updates that currently require someone to manually push them forward can run automatically.

**Business Intelligence** — Automated reporting that surfaces the information your team needs, when they need it, without requiring someone to pull and format it by hand.

**Microsoft 365 Integration** — Most of our clients are already on Microsoft's ecosystem. We build automations that extend what you already have — Teams, Outlook, SharePoint, and beyond — rather than adding yet another platform.

## Deployed on Infrastructure You Already Trust

N2M's AI services are deployed on your existing, managed infrastructure — not a separate black box. That means the same security standards, the same visibility, and the same accountability that apply to the rest of your IT environment apply here too.

Your data stays yours. Your processes stay under your control. We just make them faster.
