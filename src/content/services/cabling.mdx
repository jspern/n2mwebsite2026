## The Infrastructure That Makes Everything Else Work

Every network, every server, every camera, every phone — they all depend on what runs through your walls. Structured cabling is not glamorous, but it is foundational. And when it is done right the first time, you never have to think about it again.

N2M designs and installs cabling infrastructure built to support the demands of a modern business environment — not just what you have today, but what you'll need five years from now.

## Why Structured Cabling Matters

**Reliability** — Cheap or improperly installed cabling causes intermittent connectivity issues that are difficult to diagnose and disruptive to fix. A properly designed and certified system eliminates an entire category of problems.

**Performance** — The physical layer sets the ceiling for everything above it. Cat6A and modern fiber installations support current network speeds and have headroom for the next technology cycle.

**Scalability** — When you add workstations, cameras, access control panels, or new equipment, the infrastructure is already there to support them.

**Documentation** — Every run is labeled, every connection is documented. When something needs to change, the path forward is clear.
