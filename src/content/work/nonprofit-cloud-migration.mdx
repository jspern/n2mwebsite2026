## The Challenge

A non-profit organization was running critical donor and program management data on aging on-premise servers nearing end of life. They also had an outdated phone system that made remote work for their staff difficult. Budget constraints made a large capital investment in new hardware impractical.

## What N2M Delivered

N2M designed a cloud migration plan that moved the organization to Microsoft 365 and Azure-hosted services, eliminating the need for on-premise servers while actually improving performance and accessibility. The migration was phased to minimize disruption, with careful attention to data integrity throughout.

A new VoIP phone system replaced the legacy PBX, giving staff the ability to receive calls on mobile devices — critical for a team that frequently works in the field. Ongoing managed services ensure the environment stays secure and optimized.

## The Result

The organization eliminated its server hardware costs, gained reliable cloud infrastructure, and gave its staff the flexibility to work effectively from anywhere. "I couldn't be happier with the service provided — it was more like 10-15 miles extra!" their director reported.
