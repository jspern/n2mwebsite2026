## Communication That Keeps Up With Your Business

Traditional phone systems are expensive to maintain, inflexible to scale, and tied to a physical location. VoIP replaces all of that with modern, internet-based communication that works wherever your team works.

N2M Technology designs, deploys, and manages VoIP phone systems as part of your complete IT environment — not as a separate vendor relationship.

## What You Get

**Feature-Rich Calling** — Auto-attendants, call routing, hold music, call recording, voicemail-to-email, and the full suite of features you'd expect from an enterprise phone system — at a fraction of the cost.

**Mobile Integration** — Your office number rings on your mobile device. Your team stays reachable whether they're at their desk, working from home, or traveling.

**Scalability** — Adding a new employee or opening a new location? Adding a phone line takes minutes, not weeks.

**Cost Savings** — Eliminate per-line costs, long-distance charges, and expensive PBX hardware maintenance. VoIP typically delivers significant savings over traditional phone service.

## Integrated with Your IT Environment

Because N2M manages your entire IT environment, we configure your VoIP system to integrate cleanly with your network, security policies, and other tools. Quality of service settings ensure voice traffic is prioritized. Security policies extend to your phone system. One partner, everything connected.
