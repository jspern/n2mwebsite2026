## Physical Security Is Part of IT Security

A comprehensive IT security posture doesn't stop at the firewall. Physical access to your facility — who can enter server rooms, offices, and sensitive areas — is just as important as your network defenses. N2M Technology extends your IT environment all the way to the front door.

## Access Control

**Electronic Door Access** — Replace mechanical keys with programmable electronic credentials. Issue and revoke access instantly, without needing a locksmith.

**Credential Management** — Key cards, fobs, or mobile credentials — we configure the system, manage the credentials, and ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive areas.

**Audit Trails** — Know exactly who entered which door and when. Access logs provide accountability and are essential for compliance in many regulated industries.

## Camera Systems

**IP Camera Installation** — Modern IP cameras provide high-resolution footage that's stored, searchable, and remotely accessible. We design coverage layouts and install professional-grade systems.

**Remote Monitoring** — View live or recorded footage from anywhere, on any device. Whether you're on-site, across town, or traveling, your facility is always visible.

**Recording and Retention** — We configure recording schedules, retention periods, and storage systems to meet your needs and any applicable compliance requirements.

## One Integrated System

Because N2M manages both your IT infrastructure and your physical security systems, we can design integrated solutions — like network-connected cameras managed through the same infrastructure we support every day. One partner, one point of accountability, everything working together.
