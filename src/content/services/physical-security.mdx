## Physical Security Is Part of IT Security

A strong IT environment doesn't stop at the firewall. Who can physically access your facility — your server room, your offices, your equipment — is as important as any digital control you put in place. N2M extends your IT environment all the way to the front door.

## Access Control

**Electronic Door Access** — Replace mechanical keys with programmable electronic credentials. Issue and revoke access instantly, without a locksmith.

**Credential Management** — Key cards, fobs, or mobile credentials — we configure the system, manage the credentials, and ensure only authorized personnel reach sensitive areas.

**Audit Trails** — Know exactly who entered which door and when. Access logs provide accountability and satisfy compliance requirements in many regulated industries.

## Camera Systems

**IP Camera Installation** — Modern IP cameras provide high-resolution footage that is stored, searchable, and remotely accessible. We design coverage layouts and install professional-grade systems.

**Remote Monitoring** — View live or recorded footage from anywhere. Whether you're on-site, across town, or traveling, your facility is always visible.

**Recording and Retention** — We configure recording schedules, retention periods, and storage to meet your operational needs and any applicable compliance requirements.

## One Integrated System

Because N2M manages both your IT infrastructure and your physical security systems, we design them to work together. Network-connected cameras managed through the same infrastructure we support every day. Access control integrated with the environment we monitor continuously.

One partner. One standard. Everything working together.
