## The Right Cloud Strategy for Your Business

Cloud computing isn't one-size-fits-all. Some workloads belong in the cloud. Others run better on-premise. Most businesses benefit from a thoughtful hybrid approach. The key is knowing which is which — and having the expertise to implement it securely.

N2M Technology designs cloud environments around your business requirements, not around what's trendy or easiest to sell.

## What We Deliver

**Cloud Migration** — Moving to the cloud requires careful planning. We assess your current environment, identify what should move and when, and execute migrations with minimal disruption.

**Microsoft 365 & Azure** — As a trusted Microsoft partner ecosystem, we design, deploy, and manage the full Microsoft stack — from email and collaboration tools to enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure.

**Secure Remote Access** — Your team works from anywhere. We make sure that flexibility doesn't come at the cost of security — VPN, zero-trust access controls, and multi-factor authentication across every entry point.

**Backup & Recovery** — Cloud data still needs to be backed up. We implement and manage backup systems that ensure your cloud data is protected and recoverable.

**Cost Optimization** — Cloud costs can creep up quickly without governance. We review your cloud spend regularly and right-size resources so you're paying for what you actually use.

## Cloud That Works With Your Operations

The best cloud environment is one your team actually uses effectively. We don't just set things up and leave — we ensure your staff is comfortable with the tools, the access is right, and the environment stays optimized over time.
